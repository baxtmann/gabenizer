gabenizer
  • Revan Sopher 2014
  • Fork me on Github!

This is the website for a reddit bot which crawls /r/gentlemanboners for its top photos, runs them through facial detection, and attempts to replace every face with Gabe Newell's. The bot runs once an hour, and posts images here.

gabenizer is still under development. Content on this page is automated and may look absolutely terrible.
